# Privacy Policy for Michi

**Last Updated: July 16, 2026**

Thank you for choosing Michi ("the App"). Your privacy and mental well-being are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

## 1. No Data Collection or Transmission
Michi is designed to be a deeply personal and private space. **We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any of your personal data to external servers.** 
- There are no user accounts.
- There are no analytics trackers.
- There are no third-party advertising SDKs.

Everything you write in the App stays strictly on your device.

## 2. Local Storage
All your data—including your journal entries, meditation logs, habit streaks, and settings—is stored **100% locally** on your device using local databases (SQLite/SharedPreferences). If you uninstall the app without making a backup, your data will be permanently deleted by the operating system.

## 3. Device Permissions
Michi requests specific device permissions strictly to enable core functionality:
- **Storage Permissions (Read/Write External Storage)**: This is required exclusively for the "Auto-Backup" feature, which saves an encrypted-style backup file of your journal data directly to your device's public `Documents` folder so that it survives app uninstalls. We never read files outside of what the App creates.
- **Notifications**: Required to send you daily reminders to complete your practices. These are scheduled locally on your device and do not rely on a server.

## 4. Security (PIN Lock)
Michi offers an optional 4-digit PIN lock to protect your journal entries from physical intruders who might have access to your unlocked phone. This PIN is hashed and verified locally; it cannot be recovered by the developer if forgotten.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and mentioning the change in the App's release notes.
